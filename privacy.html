Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

OfferPilot ("we", "us") sells digital products such as The AI Job-Search Toolkit. This policy explains what data we handle and how. We keep things minimal: we do not run our own servers that store your personal data, and we never sell your data.

Who we are

OfferPilot is a small independent digital-products brand. Contact us at offerpilot.help@gmail.com.

What data we process, and why

  • Purchases. Checkout and payment are handled entirely by our marketplace partners, Gumroad and Etsy, under their own privacy policies. We receive only limited order information (such as your email and what you purchased) to deliver the product and provide support. We never see or store your full payment card details.
  • Support. If you email us, we use your message and email address only to reply and help you.
  • Pinterest. We use the Pinterest API solely to manage our own OfferPilot account — to publish and analyze our own pins. We do not access, collect, or store data about other Pinterest users.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing.
  • We do not run advertising trackers or sell profiles.
  • We do not store payment card information.

Data retention

We keep order and support information only as long as needed to deliver the product, provide support, and meet legal/accounting obligations, then delete it.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal data we hold about you by emailing offerpilot.help@gmail.com. For purchase data held by the marketplaces, please also refer to Gumroad's and Etsy's privacy policies.

Third-party services we rely on

  • Gumroad — checkout, payment, delivery.
  • Etsy — marketplace listing, checkout, payment.
  • Pinterest — marketing of our own content.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

Contact
